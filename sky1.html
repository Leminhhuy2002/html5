Sky - Children of the Light

SKY: CHILDREN OF THE LIGHT

I joined Thatgamecompany in 2014 after playing Journey and experiencing a paradigm shift about what was possible for art in games. In the following 4 years I had the pleasure of contributing to their latest release Sky: Children of the Light (Winner of iPhone Game of the Year 2019).

My tasks included World Building, Visual Style, Concept Design, and Narrative Design.
